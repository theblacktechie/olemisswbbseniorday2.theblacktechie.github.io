🏀 WNBA Draft Prospect
Lattimore is a projected 2026 WNBA Draft pick. The Women's Basketball Blog's Mock Draft 4.0 (January 2026) projects her to the Minnesota Lynx — one of the few players from the Ole Miss roster to appear in a sourced mock draft alongside McMahon.
